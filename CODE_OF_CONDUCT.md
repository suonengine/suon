# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We want Suon to be a welcoming project for everyone. Contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas in good faith.
- Give feedback that is specific, calm, and actionable.
- Be inclusive across experience levels, identities, and backgrounds.
- Respect personal boundaries, time, and communication preferences.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, trolling, doxxing, sustained disruption, or any conduct that makes participation unsafe or hostile.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and reviews
- Discord and other official community channels
- Public interactions representing the project

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainers at `ramonbernardo.contato@gmail.com`.

Please include:

- What happened
- Links or screenshots if available
- Any relevant context that can help us review the report

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they believe is appropriate to protect the community, including editing or removing content, closing discussions, rejecting contributions, temporarily restricting access, or banning participants from project spaces.

Reports will be reviewed as promptly and fairly as possible.
